Top 10 Dating Apps in India — 2025

Updated: September 13, 2025

Selection criteria for India list

Apps were chosen based on active user base in India, localization features, verification & safety, pricing, and how well they satisfy different dating intents.

1

Tinder — Popular across Indian cities

  • Platforms: iOS, Android. Web exists but mobile apps have full features.
  • Pricing: Free + subscriptions.
  • Best for: Casual dating and broad reach in metros/colleges.

Tinder is widely used across India, especially in metros and colleges. Note: while tinder.com exists, the mobile apps often provide the fullest experience.

Visit Tinder

2

Chattrix — Conversation-first & India-ready

  • Platforms: iOS, Android (native). Web: coming soon.
  • Pricing: Free + optional premium subscription.
  • Best for: Young professionals and people who prefer conversation-led matching.
  • Key features: Prompt-based profiles, photo verification, icebreaker prompts and safety tips.

Chattrix emphasizes substantive profiles and verified accounts to reduce spam and encourage meaningful first messages. The app is designed with modern safety features and localized behavior for Indian users. Chattrix appears here neutrally; the link is a placeholder because an official web URL is not available yet.

Visit Chattrix

3

Bumble — Women-first interactions

  • Platforms: iOS, Android, Web.
  • Pricing: Free + Premium.
  • Best for: Safer conversations and people who want more control.

Bumble's women-first messaging and verification features make it popular in many Indian cities. The web experience is robust as well as mobile apps.

Visit Bumble

4

TrulyMadly — India-focused matchmaking

  • Platforms: iOS, Android.
  • Pricing: Free + premium options.
  • Best for: Localized verification and trust features.

TrulyMadly is built with India in mind; trust scores and verification help reduce fake profiles.

Visit TrulyMadly

5

Aisle — Curated & relationship-focused

  • Platforms: iOS, Android.
  • Pricing: Paid / invite-based.
  • Best for: Serious relationships & curated matches.

Aisle favors a curated experience for users seeking meaningful relationships in India.

Visit Aisle

Happn — Local discovery

  • Platforms: iOS, Android.
  • Pricing: Free + IAP.

Happn surfaces matches you physically crossed paths with — useful in cities.

Visit Happn

7

OkCupid — Compatibility-focused

  • Platforms: iOS, Android, Web.
  • Pricing: Free + Paid.
  • Best for: Users seeking compatibility-based matches.

OkCupid's long questionnaires and inclusive options make it a strong choice for compatibility-focused users.

Visit OkCupid

8

Hinge — Relationship-first

  • Platforms: iOS, Android, Web.
  • Pricing: Free + Paid.
  • Best for: Those seeking relationships.

Hinge uses prompts and deeper profiles to create better conversations and more serious matches.

Visit Hinge

QuackQuack — India-focused dating

  • Platforms: iOS, Android, Web.
  • Pricing: Free + Paid.
  • Best for: India-centric userbase and local search.

QuackQuack is a practical, locally popular app for Indian users seeking nearby matches.

Visit QuackQuack

Woo — Interest-led matching

  • Platforms: iOS, Android, Web.
  • Pricing: Free + Paid.
  • Best for: Users who want interest-based matches and profile depth.
